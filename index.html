What is Robotics?

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that combines elements of engineering, computer science, and technology to design, build, and operate robots. Robots are programmable machines capable of performing tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously. The field of robotics involves a wide range of applications, from manufacturing and medical procedures to space exploration and entertainment.

Why Robotics?

Robotics is transforming industries and everyday life by introducing automation and intelligent systems. By replacing or augmenting human capabilities, robots can enhance precision, efficiency, and safety in various tasks. Robotics also opens up new possibilities for innovation, from exploring remote environments to developing advanced healthcare solutions. As technology advances, robotics will continue to play a critical role in solving complex problems and improving the quality of life.
